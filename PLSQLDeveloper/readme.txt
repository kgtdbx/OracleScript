To copy necessaries settings do the following:
 
Go to C:\Users\<user>\AppData\Roaming\PLSQL Developer 12\Preferences\<user> (e.g. C:\Users\serhiib\AppData\Roaming\PLSQL Developer 12\Preferences\serhiib)
and copy default.ini and user.prefs files.

And it will be enough to replace them for new version.
